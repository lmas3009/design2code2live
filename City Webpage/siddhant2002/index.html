Welcome to Odisha State Housing Board

The Odisha State Housing Board came in to existence in the year 1968 by an Act of State Legislature with prime objective of providing Affordable houses in urban and rural areas to cater to the acute shortage of housing in the state.

Our Services

Simplex

Small size properties best suited for small families at affordable rates.

Duplex

Multi-family homes that have two units in the same building and share a wall between each other.

Apartments

2BHK and 3BHK flats available at affordable rates.
